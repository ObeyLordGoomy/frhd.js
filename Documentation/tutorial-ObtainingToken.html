Obtaining Token

To obtain your token go to FreeRider and follow these steps

  1. On your keyboard hit "Ctrl" + "Shift" + "J" at the same time
  2. Click onto the "Application" tab
  3. In the "Storage" section click "Cookies" then click "https://www.freeriderhd.com"
  4. Double click in the "Value" box next to "frhd_app_sr" and copy your token
